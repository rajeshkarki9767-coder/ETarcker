Common Issues — Quick Answers

My data disappeared

All data is stored in your browser's localStorage. If you cleared browser data, cache, or site storage, this permanently deletes your expenses. Prevention: export a JSON backup weekly via Settings → Data backup → Export JSON.

Ads are not showing

Ads require an internet connection. If you have an ad blocker active or are in private/incognito mode, ads will not load. The app works fully without ads — they only appear when you're online.

AI chat is not working

The AI assistant requires an active internet connection and may be temporarily unavailable during server maintenance. All other app features work fully offline.

I forgot my PIN

There is no PIN recovery — this is intentional to protect your privacy. You will need to reset the app in Settings → Reset all data. Export your data first if possible.

How do I move data to a new device?

Settings → Data backup → Export JSON on your old device. Transfer the file (email it to yourself, save to Google Drive, etc.). Then open E Tracker on the new device and go to Settings → Data backup → Import JSON.

Before You Contact Support

  • Check the How to Use guide — most common questions are answered there
  • Include your device type and browser/app version in bug reports
  • Screenshots are very helpful for UI issues
  • For data loss: note when it happened and whether you had a backup

About Our Privacy Practices

Data privacy contact

If you have questions specifically about how your data is handled, how Google AdSense cookies work on our platform, or if you would like to exercise any data rights available in your jurisdiction, please contact us at privacy@etracker.app.

We will respond to privacy-related enquiries within 5 business days. For European users with GDPR-related requests, we aim to respond within the legally required 30 days.

Advertising questions

E Tracker uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. If you have questions about the advertising displayed, including how to opt out of personalised ads, visit Google Ads Settings. Opting out of personalised advertising will result in generic ads being displayed instead — the app functionality is not affected.

Feedback and Feature Requests

E Tracker is continuously improved based on user feedback. If there is a feature you would like to see — a new analytics view, a different way to categorise expenses, support for a specific payment method or currency — we want to hear about it. Most of the features currently in the app were added because users requested them.

The best way to submit feature requests is email at feedback@etracker.app. We read every message, though we cannot guarantee every feature will be implemented.

Why E Tracker is Free

E Tracker is completely free to use with no subscription fees, no premium tier, and no features locked behind a paywall. Every capability — expense tracking, analytics, AI insights, PIN protection, data export — is available to all users at no cost.

The app is funded through Google AdSense advertising. When you use E Tracker, you may see a small banner advertisement and occasional interstitial advertisements between actions. These are served by Google and may be personalised based on your browsing history using cookies. This is clearly disclosed in our Privacy Policy.

We chose this funding model because it allows us to offer the app for free to everyone, particularly students and young professionals in Nepal and South Asia who are building their financial habits. We believe financial awareness tools should be accessible regardless of income.

Accessibility and Language

E Tracker is currently available in English only. The app is designed to work on all modern mobile and desktop browsers without installation. If you require assistance in Nepali or another language, or if you have accessibility requirements that the current interface does not meet, please contact us at support@etracker.app and we will do our best to assist you directly.
