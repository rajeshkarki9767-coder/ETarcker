Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025  |  Effective: January 2025

🔒 Your privacy is our core principle

E Tracker is built on a simple promise: your financial data belongs to you. We do not collect it, we do not store it on servers, and we do not sell it. Ever. This policy explains exactly what data exists, where it lives, and how we handle the one exception (advertising).

1. Who We Are

E Tracker — Personal Expense Tracking Application

E Tracker is a free personal finance web application and Progressive Web App (PWA) available at etracker.app and related domains. The application is designed to help individuals track personal expenses, analyse spending patterns, and build better financial habits.

For privacy questions, contact: privacy@etracker.app

3. Google AdSense and Advertising

How advertising works in E Tracker Third-party cookies may apply

E Tracker displays advertisements served by Google AdSense (Publisher ID: ca-pub-7563052888132138). Google AdSense may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to serve personalised advertisements based on your interests.

What Google may collect through ads

Google AdSense uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — and similar tracking technologies including web beacons and pixel tags to:

  • Serve personalised advertisements based on your browsing history and interests
  • Track browsing behaviour across websites that participate in Google's advertising network
  • Collect device type, browser version, and operating system information
  • Record approximate geographic location at country and city level
  • Measure ad performance: impressions, clicks, and conversion events
  • Prevent ad fraud and ensure ads are shown to real users

These cookies may persist on your device for up to 13 months. Google's advertising ID cookies include: IDE (DoubleClick), DSID, FLC, AID, TAID, and NID. For a complete list see Google's cookie types.

Legal basis for advertising cookies

In jurisdictions covered by GDPR (European Economic Area), Google's advertising cookies are processed on the basis of legitimate interest or, where required, consent. Users in these regions may see a consent banner. For users in other regions including Nepal, cookies are set automatically as part of the advertising service we use to fund the free app.

Your advertising choices

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info. You can also use your browser's private/incognito mode to prevent ad-related cookies from persisting.

For full details on Google's data practices, see: Google Privacy Policy and Google Advertising Policy.

4. AI Assistant Feature (Optional)

What happens when you use Ask AI Sends data to Anthropic

The AI chat feature is completely optional and disabled by default. When you choose to use it, the following occurs:

  • A summary of your expense records (dates, amounts, categories — no personal identifiers) is sent to Anthropic's Claude API
  • Anthropic processes this data to generate a response to your question
  • The data is processed according to Anthropic's Privacy Policy

We recommend reviewing Anthropic's privacy policy before using the AI feature if you have concerns about data sharing. You can use all other features of E Tracker without ever using the AI assistant.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

E Tracker itself does not set any first-party cookies. All app data is stored in browser localStorage, which is a separate mechanism from cookies and is not accessible to third parties or transmitted over the network.

However, Google AdSense sets third-party cookies on your device when you view or interact with advertisements. The following table summarises the cookies you may receive:

CookiePurposeDuration

Managing cookies: You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. In Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies. In Safari: Settings → Safari → Privacy. Blocking advertising cookies will not affect E Tracker's core functionality but will result in non-personalised (generic) ads instead of targeted ones.

Opt out of personalised advertising: Visit Google Ad Settings, aboutads.info, or Network Advertising Initiative to opt out of interest-based advertising.

6. Data Security

Your expense data is protected by being stored locally on your device — only you have access to it. No transmission means no interception risk.

For additional security, E Tracker offers an optional PIN lock feature that encrypts access to the app. We recommend enabling this if your device is shared.

Note: Clearing your browser's localStorage, cache, or site data will permanently delete your expense records. We strongly recommend using the Export JSON backup feature regularly.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over retention and deletion:

  • Delete individual expenses: Swipe left on any expense in the list
  • Delete all data: Settings → Reset all data & settings
  • Delete the app: Clear site data in your browser settings

When you delete data, it is immediately and permanently removed. There is no server backup we need to delete — the data never existed anywhere else.

8. Children's Privacy

E Tracker is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@etracker.app.

9. International Users

E Tracker is available worldwide. Since your data is stored locally on your device, it is not transferred internationally by us. However, if you use the AI assistant or interact with Google AdSense, data may be processed in the United States or other countries where Anthropic and Google operate.
