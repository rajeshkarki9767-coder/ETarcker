E Tracker - Free Personal Expense Tracker App

E Tracker is a free, privacy-first expense tracking app. Track your daily spending, analyse your budget, and build better financial habits. All your expense data is stored locally on your device — never on any server.

Features

  • Add and manage daily expenses with categories
  • Visual spending analytics and charts
  • Monthly and yearly spending summaries
  • Multiple payment method tracking
  • Data export and backup
  • PIN lock for privacy
  • Dark and light theme
  • Works offline as a Progressive Web App

Privacy & Data
How E Tracker handles your data
🔒
100% Private by Design
Your expense data never leaves your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking — ever.
Data Storage
Stored only on this device
All expenses, categories, and settings are saved in your browser's localStorage — only on this device, only in this browser.
No internet required
The app works fully offline. No data is ever transmitted to any server (except when you choose to use the AI assistant).
You own your data
Export a full backup as JSON anytime. Import it on another device. Delete everything instantly with the Reset option in Settings.
What is stored locally
Expense records Local only
Date, amount, vendor name, category, payment method, tags, notes
App settings Local only
Currency symbol, custom categories, payment methods, PIN (hashed)
Nothing else Never collected
No name, email, phone, location, device ID, or usage analytics
AI Assistant
Optional & opt-in only
The AI chat feature is completely optional. When you use it, a summary of your expense data is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to answer your questions.
What is sent to the API
Your expense records (dates, amounts, categories, vendor names) are included in the AI prompt. No personal identifiers are sent.
Third-party policy
Data sent to the AI is subject to Anthropic's privacy policy. We recommend not using the AI feature if you have privacy concerns about third-party APIs.
PIN Security
PIN is stored locally
Your PIN is saved only in your browser's localStorage. It is never transmitted anywhere. If you forget your PIN, you can reset all data from Settings to regain access.
Clearing browser data removes the PIN
Clearing your browser's site data or localStorage will erase all app data including the PIN. Always export a backup before clearing browser data.
Deleting Your Data
Reset from Settings
Use Settings → Reset to permanently delete all expenses and settings from this device instantly.
Clear browser storage
You can also clear all data by clearing this site's localStorage in your browser's developer tools or privacy settings.
E Tracker is a local-first personal finance tool. We believe your financial data is deeply personal and belongs only to you.
