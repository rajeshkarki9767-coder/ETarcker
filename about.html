Our Story

Why we built E Tracker

Managing personal finances in Nepal is harder than it should be. Most apps either require an account and cloud sync, charge a monthly fee, send your private financial data to servers you don't control, or are so complex they take more time than they save.

We wanted something different. A tool that respects your privacy, works offline, loads instantly, and makes logging an expense as fast as possible. So we built E Tracker.

Built in Nepal, for the world

E Tracker was designed with the Nepali context in mind — NPR currency, familiar payment methods like Cash, Bank, Khalti, eSewa, and spending categories that reflect real Nepali life. But it works for anyone, anywhere, in any currency.

What Makes E Tracker Different

vs. Bank apps

Bank apps only show transactions from one account. E Tracker works across all your cash, cards, wallets, and digital payment methods — giving you a complete picture of spending regardless of how you pay.

vs. Cloud finance apps (Mint, YNAB, etc.)

Cloud apps require accounts and store your data on their servers. If they shut down, your data is gone. E Tracker stores everything locally — your data is always yours, even offline, even if we disappeared tomorrow.

vs. Spreadsheets

Spreadsheets are powerful but require manual work and give no visual insights automatically. E Tracker gives you charts, trends, and AI-powered analysis automatically, with data entry that takes seconds per transaction.

How E Tracker Works

100% local storage — your data never leaves your device

E Tracker stores all expense records in your browser's localStorage — a secure storage area built into every modern browser. This data is sandboxed to E Tracker's domain and is technically inaccessible to any website, server, or third party. When you add an expense, the data is written instantly to localStorage on your device. No network request is made. No server receives anything.

This architecture means the app works completely offline, loads instantly from cache on repeat visits, and provides genuine privacy — not just a privacy promise.

Advertising transparency

E Tracker is free and supported by Google AdSense advertising. Ads appear as a small banner at the top of the content area and occasionally as interstitial overlays between actions. Google AdSense may use cookies to personalise ads based on your browsing history. See our Privacy Policy for full details on which cookies are set and how to opt out.

We are transparent about advertising because we believe you have the right to know how a free service is funded. We chose AdSense because it is a reputable, well-regulated advertising platform with clear opt-out mechanisms.
